Explore the Rich Legacy of Our Institution

Our Journey Through Time

Homagama Maha Vidyalaya, established in 1920, has grown from humble beginnings into one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Sri Lanka. Our school has a proud history of academic excellence and a commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals.

Starting as a small village school, we have expanded over the decades to accommodate thousands of students from diverse backgrounds. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated staff have continuously propelled us to new heights.

The legacy of our school is built on the pillars of discipline, knowledge, and service to society. From its inception, Homagama Maha Vidyalaya has been a beacon of hope and opportunity for generations of students.

Join us as we celebrate over a century of excellence, and continue to strive towards an even brighter future.
